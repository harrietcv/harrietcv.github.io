Adjectives

I heard a Californian student in Heidelberg say, in one of his calmest moods, that he would rather decline two drinks than one German adjective.

Adjectives qualify nouns in German.

What does 'qualify' mean?

  • If placed before the noun, their ending reflects the gender, number (singular or plural) and case of the noun. Example: kaltes Wasser
  • If placed after the noun, they act as a 'complement' - they have no ending, but they describe the noun a little. Example: Das Wasser ist kalt Wasser
